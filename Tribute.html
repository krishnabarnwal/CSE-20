Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

Biography

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

Key Achievements

  • Project Director of India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III).
  • Played a pivotal role in the Pokhran-II nuclear tests.
  • Author of 'Wings of Fire' and 'Ignited Minds'.
